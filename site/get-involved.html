Other ways to help

  • Print a campaign poster and put it up on a campus, hostel, or community noticeboard.
  • Suggest this site to a student club, RWA, or college wellness cell in your part of Bangalore.
  • If you're an educator or counselor, use the Effects and Myths vs Facts pages as talking-point references.
  • If you spot something on this site that needs a correction, flag it to whoever's maintaining the campaign — accuracy is the whole point.
