Why this site exists

Weed is easy to find in Bangalore's college and party scene — and easy to misjudge.

Most young people don't decide about weed based on a lecture — they decide based on what their friends say. A lot of what gets repeated is wrong, outdated, or half-true. This site exists to close that gap with straight information, not moralizing.

25
The age your brain typically finishes developing. Regular use before then hits memory and focus hardest.
Not myth
Regular use can lead to real dependency (Cannabis Use Disorder) — despite one of the most common claims you'll hear.
1985
The year India's NDPS Act made ganja/weed illegal nationwide — still true today, in Bangalore too.
Slower
Reaction time, memory, and focus — measurably impaired even at "just a little" use.
